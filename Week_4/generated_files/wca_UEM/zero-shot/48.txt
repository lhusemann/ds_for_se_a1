The `TermVectorsWriter` class is an abstract class used for writing term vectors in Lucene. It provides methods for starting and finishing documents, fields, and terms, as well as adding positions, offsets, and payloads. The `merge` method is used for merging term vectors from multiple readers. The `addAllDocVectors` method is a safe but slow default method for writing every vector field in the document. The class also implements the `Closeable` and `Accountable` interfaces.
