**Title:** PointsFormat — Encoding and Decoding of Indexed Points

**Summary:**
The PointsFormat class is a key component of the Lucene library, responsible for encoding and decoding indexed points. It provides two main methods: `fieldsWriter` and `fieldsReader`. The `fieldsWriter` method is used to create a writer for a new segment, while the `fieldsReader` method is used to read a segment.

The writer is responsible for writing the points to the segment, while the reader is responsible for reading the points from the segment. The writer and reader are both stateful and depend on the `SegmentWriteState` and `SegmentReadState` objects, respectively.

The PointsFormat class is designed to be pluggable, allowing different implementations to be used depending on the specific requirements of the application. It is part of the Lucene library, which is used for full-text search and indexing.
