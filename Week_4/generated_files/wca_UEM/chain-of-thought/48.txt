**Title:** TermVectorsWriter: A Lucene Library Class for Writing Term Vectors

**Summary:**
The TermVectorsWriter class is a part of the Lucene library that provides functionality for writing term vectors. Term vectors are sequences of terms and their associated attributes such as positions, offsets, and payloads. The class has methods for starting and finishing documents, fields, and terms, as well as adding positions, offsets, and payloads. It also includes methods for finishing the writing process and merging term vectors from multiple readers.

The TermVectorsWriter class implements the Closeable and Accountable interfaces, which provide methods for closing the writer and estimating the memory usage of the object.

The class has a constructor that is called when an object is created. It also has abstract methods that must be implemented by any subclass, including startDocument, startField, startTerm, addPosition, finish, and close.

The TermVectorsWriter class is intended for use in software engineering and specifically for writing term vectors in the context of Lucene. It is complex and includes many low-level details related to term vector writing and merging.
