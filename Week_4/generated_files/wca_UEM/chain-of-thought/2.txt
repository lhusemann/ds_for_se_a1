**Title:** SimpleTextSkipWriter and SimpleTextSkipReader
**Summary:** The SimpleTextSkipWriter class is used to write skip data in a plain text format for a Lucene index. It buffers skip data and writes it to an output stream, optimizing the retrieval of documents in the index. The SimpleTextSkipReader class is a reader for Lucene's SimpleTextFieldsWriter. It reads in the fields from the input and returns them as a Terms object. The Terms object can be iterated over to retrieve the terms and their associated information, such as doc frequency, total term frequency, and postings. The postings can be iterated over to retrieve the doc IDs, term frequencies, positions, offsets, and payloads. The SimpleTextFieldsReader also handles skip lists, which are used to efficiently navigate through the postings. The skip lists contain information about the postings at certain intervals, allowing for faster navigation to specific terms. The SimpleTextFieldsReader also handles terms enumerations, which allow for efficient navigation through the terms in the index.
