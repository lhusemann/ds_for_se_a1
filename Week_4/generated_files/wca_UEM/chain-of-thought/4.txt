**Title:** Utility methods for reading and writing versioned headers
**Summary:** The code provides utility methods for reading and writing versioned headers, which are used to identify the format of a file and ensure it is in the expected format. It includes methods for writing a codec header, an index header, and a codec footer, as well as methods for reading and validating headers and footers. It also includes constants for the magic numbers used in headers and footers, as well as helper methods for reading and writing big-endian integers and longs.
