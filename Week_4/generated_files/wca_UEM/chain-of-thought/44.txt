**Title:** StoredFieldsWriter in Lucene
**Summary:** The StoredFieldsWriter class is responsible for writing stored fields in a Lucene index. It provides methods for writing and merging stored fields, as well as finishing and closing the writer. The merge() method iterates over the documents in multiple readers and calls startDocument(), writeField(), and finishDocument() for each document. The class has dependencies on various classes from the Lucene library, such as FieldInfo, IndexableField, MergeState, and TokenStream. It extends the Closeable and Accountable interfaces.
