**Title:** TermVectorsFormat Class in Lucene Library
**Summary:** The TermVectorsFormat class is used to control the format of term vectors in the Lucene library. It provides abstract methods for reading and writing term vectors, which are sequences of terms and their associated information. Subclasses can implement their own logic for these operations by providing concrete implementations of the vectorsReader and vectorsWriter methods. The class itself does not have any concrete methods or fields, but rather provides a framework for subclasses to implement their own logic for reading and writing term vectors.
