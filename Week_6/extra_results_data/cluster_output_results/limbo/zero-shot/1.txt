Title: Lucene Terms Dictionary Reader

Components and Interactions:
- TermsDictReader: Reads terms from a Lucene index and provides an iterator to iterate through the terms.
- PostingsReaderBase: Handles the reading of doc/freqs and positions from a Lucene index.
- TermsIndexReaderBase: Abstracts away the specific implementation of the terms dict index, allowing for flexibility in the implementation of the terms dictionary.
