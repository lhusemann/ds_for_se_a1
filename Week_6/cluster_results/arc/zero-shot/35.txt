The FieldsConsumer class is an abstract class that implements the Closeable interface and is used for consuming terms, doc, freq, prox, offset, and payloads postings. It has a constructor and several methods for writing and merging fields. The write method takes a Fields instance and a NormsProducer and is responsible for writing all fields, terms, and postings. The merge method takes a MergeState and a NormsProducer and is responsible for merging in the fields from the readers in the MergeState. The close method is overridden from the Closeable interface and is used to close the FieldsConsumer.
