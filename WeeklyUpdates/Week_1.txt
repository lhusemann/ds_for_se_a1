So in total, we compiled the Lucene-codecs-9.0.0 because Lucene-codecs-11.0.0 couldnt be supported with Arcade.
Extracted the master .rsf and .fv. In our Github Repo they are called output.rsf and output.fv.
On this output.rsf we applied a filter to get filtered.rsf. It was filtered to extract the given target packages.
TARGET_PACKAGES = [
    "org.apache.lucene.codecs.simpletext",
    "org.apache.lucene.codecs.blockterms",
    "org.apache.lucene.codecs.bloom",
    "org.apache.lucene.codecs.memory",
    "org.apache.lucene.codecs.blocktreeords",
]
And finally we applied the WCA Limbo and ACDC Algorithms on them.
