**Title:** Lucene Norms Format
**Summary:** The Lucene Norms Format is a class used for encoding and decoding per-document score normalization values. It has two abstract methods, normsConsumer and normsProducer, which are used for writing and reading norms to and from the index, respectively. The class takes SegmentReadState and SegmentWriteState objects as input, which contain information about the segments being read or written. The NormsFormat class is responsible for managing the state of the segments and providing methods for writing and reading norms to and from the index.
